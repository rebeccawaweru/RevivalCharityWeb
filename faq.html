Frequently Asked Questions

How can I get involved?

You can make donations to various fundraisers in your community or volunteer for activities to help build your community

How do I maximize my reach in the community?

You can use email invitations and sms broadcast features and also share your projects to other social media platforms using links

Why is this platform important?

This platform's concentration has been narrowed down to your own local community directing all resource to the development of everyone within that community

How do I keep in touch with my donors or volunteers?

Login and click on the More dropdown. You will see My Projects button. Click the button to view your fundraisers,acivities, donors and volunteers

How do I ask a question or send feedback?

Go to About us page, scroll down and fill in the contact form. You will get our feedback via email after submission.
